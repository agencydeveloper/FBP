Reporting.

1st step: ATTENTION/ATTENTION ON DECK command will be called

2nd step: officer/WO will say someone name and ask to report (e.g officer: Airgod PROnoob report!)

3rd step: the reporter will say him/her rank and Reporting as ordered sir/ma'am! (e.g Airgod: Airgod PROnoob reporting as ordered sir!)

4th step: officer will demote/promote/reward/warn/discharge you.

5th step: then officer will say "Dismissed!"

6th step: you will say Sir/ma'am!

7th step: you will wait officer say carry on/at ease

8th step: then you can continue what you were doing
